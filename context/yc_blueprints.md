# YC Startup Blueprints: Alpha Insights

## Harbinger: Real Estate Predictive Analytics
- **Concept**: Two-year-ahead neighborhood map for real estate investors.
- **Problem**: Zillow signals are lagging. By the time a zip code is marked "hot", prices have already moved.
- **Proprietary Signals**: Transit announcements, zoning changes, permit filings eighteen months before they hit mainstream comparable sales.
- **Actionable Advice**: Validate the model by backtesting municipal planning APIs against historical appreciation. Focus on depth (one city) before breadth.

## Fitproof: Product-Market Fit Scoring
- **Concept**: Automated PMF scoring tool that weights engagement, retention, and sentiment.
- **Moat**: Comparative benchmarks across hundreds of startups at similar stages.
- **Strategic Insight**: Weighting must be calibrated against founder intuition before investor expectations. If the score contradicts lived reality, it lacks credibility.
- **Growth Strategy**: Partner with accelerators where PMF pressure is highest.

## Prequal: Construction Compliance Watchdog
- **Concept**: Automated safety and certification tracking for mid-market general contractors.
- **Problem**: Compliance lapses in spreadsheets lead to job shutdowns and massive liability.
- **Execution**: Connect directly to public OSHA violation databases and state credential registries. 
- **Selling Point**: The delta between what the system caught and what the spreadsheets missed.
