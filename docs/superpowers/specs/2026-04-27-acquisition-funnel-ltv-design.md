# Customer Acquisition Funnel & LTV Model — The Builder Brief

**Date:** 2026-04-27  
**Scope:** Free → Pro → Max → Incubator progression, upgrade triggers, LTV targets, churn mitigation  
**Goal:** Maximize Free→Pro conversion while minimizing churn from paid tiers

---

## Overview

The Builder Brief uses an **outcome-based funnel** mapped to founder journey stages. Each tier unlocks progressively deeper access to founder knowledge and network. Positioning is authentic founder voice (no marketing jargon), specific to their traction level and market.

---

## Tier Architecture

### **Free Tier: Discovery Phase**

**What it is:** Founder realizes something's broken in their business.

**Core feature:** Scorecard (health diagnostic). Shows actual metrics broken down by what matters. One weekly insight (market trend or tactical tip).

**Activation event:** First scorecard run.

**Churn signal:** Haven't run scorecard in 2 weeks = lost.

**LTV:** Not revenue-based. Success metric = 10-15% convert to Pro within 3-6 month window.

---

### **Pro Tier: Direction Phase**

**What it is:** Founder knows what's broken, needs direction to fix it.

**Price:** $300/year = $25/month.

**Unlocks:**
- AI context engine (reads their specific situation: stage, traction, market, competitive landscape)
- Playbook (how founders in their stage solved similar problems)
- Founder chat/calls (ask questions to founders who've done it)

**Positioning:** "You're not lost. Here's the map."

**Upgrade trigger:** Weak health score + 3+ attempts to unlock playbook sections.

**LTV target:** $600 (24-month retention = 2x first year). Implies 50% stick to year 2.

**Churn rate:** ~5% monthly.

**Churn mitigation:**
- Hyper-personalization. Every tip, every call recommendation, every insight is specific to their traction level + market + stage.
- Generic advice = death. If it feels like generic SaaS content, they churn immediately.
- Weekly insights tied to their specific metrics (not random market news).
- Founder calls matched to their stage (seed-stage founder talks to seed-stage founder, not Series A).

---

### **Max Tier: Acceleration Phase**

**What it is:** Founder has direction, needs speed and strategic depth.

**Price:** $5,000/year = $416/month.

**Unlocks (on top of Pro):**
- Direct 1:1 calls with founders who've exited (quarterly deep dives on their specific competitive landscape and go-to-market strategy)
- Custom quarterly strategy sessions (build your next 90-day roadmap with someone who's been there)
- Deeper competitive analysis (quarterly deep dives, not ad-hoc)

**Positioning:** "You're not guessing anymore. You're executing with a scar-tissue mentor."

**Upgrade trigger:** Hit 2 of 3 Pro milestones:
- Revenue target hit (e.g., $500 MRR)
- User count target hit (e.g., 500 active users)
- Feature shipped (shipped first feature from playbook)

**LTV target:** $15,000 (36-month retention = 3 years minimum). Implies 67% stick through year 3.

**Churn rate:** ~2-3% monthly.

**Churn mitigation:**
- Relationship lock-in. Founder has 1-2 assigned advisors they know and trust (not rotating roster).
- Switching costs are high: founder has already built strategic roadmap with their advisor, changing advisors = restart.
- Quarterly check-ins are mandatory (part of the tier). Keeps founder engaged even in slow months.
- Advisor is incentivized to see them succeed (potential equity in Incubator tier if they graduate).

---

### **Incubator Tier: Validation Phase**

**What it is:** Founder is executing well, trajectory is clear. Scout identifies them.

**Price:** Invitation-only. Assumed $120k+/year or equity stake (not defined yet; TBD based on pricing model).

**Unlocks (on top of Max):**
- Capital introductions (investors in the Builder Brief network)
- Strategic partnerships (intro to portfolio companies who could be customers/partners/acquirers)
- Curated founder peer group (founders 6-12 months ahead in their journey for peer learning)
- Potential equity stake in their company (Builder Brief takes 0.5-2% for continued support and network access)

**Positioning:** "You've proven it works. Now we're putting money and network behind you."

**Upgrade trigger:** Scout invite. Triggered by:
- Traction signal (hitting Max tier milestones consistently, 2+ consecutive quarters of growth)
- Founder credibility signal (founder background, previous exits, market validation, team quality)
- Market opportunity signal (TAM, defensibility, timing)

**LTV target:** $40,000+ (48+ months minimum, potentially lifetime). Implies <1% monthly churn.

**Churn rate:** <1% monthly.

**Churn mitigation:**
- Selectivity = commitment. Being invited (not asked to apply) creates psychological investment.
- Capital + network + equity = real ROI. Founder has structural incentive to stay.
- Founder community (peer learning) creates additional stickiness beyond 1:1 advisor relationship.
- Exit optionality: Founder can exit after Series A (cash out equity stake) or stay indefinitely.

## Conversion Mechanics

### **Free → Pro Conversion**

**Trigger events:**
- Scorecard shows critical weakness (e.g., health score <40)
- 3+ clicks on locked playbook sections
- 2 weeks of consistent scorecard usage

**Messaging:** "Ready to fix this? Playbook + market intel + founder calls unlock here."

**Expected conversion rate:** 10-15% of Free signups within 3-6 months.

**Conversion window:** 3-6 months from signup. After 6 months without conversion, assume churn.

---

### **Pro → Max Conversion**

**Trigger events:**
- Hit 2 of 3 milestones:
  - Revenue target ($500 MRR minimum)
  - User count target (500+ active users)
  - Shipped first playbook-derived feature
- Email offer timed to milestone achievement (within 1 week of hitting goal)

**Messaging:** "You've proven the playbook works. Now accelerate with founder calls and custom strategy."

**Expected conversion rate:** 20-30% of Pro users (those who hit milestones).

**Conversion window:** Within 6 months of Pro signup. By month 18-24 (Max LTV threshold), 50% of Pro users should have either upgraded or churned.

---

### **Max → Incubator Invitation**

**Trigger:** Scout identification based on:
- 2+ consecutive quarters of growth (traction signal)
- Founder credibility (background, network, domain expertise)
- Market opportunity (TAM >$1B, defensible moat)
- Founder health (personal financial stability, commitment to founder role, no red flags)

**Messaging:** "You're in the top 5% of founders we work with. We want to back you."

**Expected conversion rate:** 3-5% of Max tier (invitation-only by design).

**Conversion window:** Month 12-24 of Max tenure (once founder has proven trajectory).

## Churn Risk Framework (All Tiers)

### **Universal Churn Risks**
1. **Generic advice** → Founder sees content that could apply to any startup. Dies immediately.
2. **No perceived value** → Tips/calls don't match their specific stage, market, or traction level.
3. **Information too generic** → Market intel is publicly available elsewhere, not proprietary Builder Brief insight.
4. **Startup failure** (uncontrollable) → Founder runs out of runway. Likely churn but not preventable.

### **Universal Churn Mitigation**
- **Hyper-personalization:** Every recommendation, insight, and advisor match is specific to their:
  - Traction level (seed vs. Series A vs. Series B)
  - Market (B2B SaaS, marketplace, consumer, etc.)
  - Competitive landscape (industry-specific vulnerabilities and opportunities)
  - Stage-specific playbook (seed founders get seed-founder calls, not Series A)
- **AI context engine:** Reads their scorecard, recent metrics, and founder profile to personalize recommendations in real-time.

### **Pro-Specific Churn Risks**
- Advisor calls feel generic or unrelated to their situation
- Playbook is too broad (not specific enough to their market/stage)
- Weekly insights are obvious/publicly available

### **Pro-Specific Mitigation**
- Advisor rosters are curated by stage and market (seed-stage B2B SaaS founders get seed-stage B2B SaaS advisors)
- Playbook sections are tagged by stage + market so they see relevant content only
- Weekly insights are proprietary (founder-exclusive market analysis, not repurposed blog posts)

### **Max-Specific Churn Risks**
- Assigned advisor changes or leaves (relationship loss)
- Quarterly strategy session feels like a sales call, not strategic collaboration
- Competitive analysis is stale (quarterly not frequent enough)

### **Max-Specific Mitigation**
- Advisor continuity guarantee (same 1-2 advisors for 12+ months)
- Strategy sessions are founder-led (they set the agenda, advisor helps pressure-test)
- Competitive analysis is real-time (AI context engine flagged competitive threats immediately, not just quarterly)

## Success Metrics

**Free tier:**
- Signup rate
- Scorecard completion rate (activation)
- Weekly insight engagement
- Conversion rate to Pro (target: 10-15% within 6 months)

**Pro tier:**
- Activation rate (first founder call booked)
- Playbook engagement (pages viewed, features shipped from playbook)
- Monthly churn rate (target: ~5%)
- LTV achievement (target: $600 by month 24)
- Conversion rate to Max (target: 20-30% of those hitting milestones)

**Max tier:**
- Quarterly strategy session completion rate
- Competitive analysis engagement
- Monthly churn rate (target: ~2-3%)
- LTV achievement (target: $15,000 by month 36)
- Incubator invitation rate (target: 3-5%)

**Incubator tier:**
- Time to Series A / Series B (external success metric)
- Monthly churn rate (target: <1%)
- Network value delivered (partnerships, intros, capital)
- Exit outcomes (acquisition, IPO, etc.)

## Positioning Principles (All Tiers)

- **No marketing jargon.** Write like a serial founder with exits, not a content marketing team.
- **Stage-specific language.** Seed founders hear "find product-market fit," Series A founders hear "scale repeatable growth."
- **Authentic founder voice.** Everything sounds like it came from someone who's been there, not a corporate brand.
- **Zero placeholder metrics.** All examples and data are real or explicitly marked "example."
- **Network credibility.** Emphasize founder-to-founder connections, not algorithmic matching or AI magic.
